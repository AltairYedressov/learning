01. About Me

Infrastructure & Platform Engineer based in New York, NY — I build the systems that let engineers ship faster, sleep better, and spend less.

With over 5 years of hands-on experience, I manage large-scale Kubernetes environments, build self-service CI/CD pipelines, and automate cloud infrastructure with Terraform — from cluster provisioning to policy enforcement.

At Figma, I manage 15+ EKS clusters and 20+ microservices, building deployment pipelines that serve 30+ engineers — cutting release cycles from days to hours. Previously at FanDuel, I containerized legacy services, built observability stacks, and architected multi-region failover systems.

I'm passionate about GitOps, observability, cost optimization, and building platforms that let engineering teams ship faster with confidence.

Away from the terminal, not much changes — I still need to see a thing go from zero to done. I built a sauna in my backyard, I do my own home repairs, and I'm the guy under the car on a Saturday trying to figure out why that one bolt won't come off. I hit up tech conferences whenever I can because I genuinely hate feeling behind on what's new. And honestly, the best part of my week is hanging out with my two nieces — they don't care about Kubernetes at all, which is probably why it's so refreshing.
